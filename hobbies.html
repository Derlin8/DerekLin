My Hobbies and Interests

Right now, I devote most of my time to coursework and endurance running. I run track and cross country for the NCAA team here at UC Davis in addition to pursuing my bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

This is my third year at UC Davis and I am incredibly grateful to have met great friends through my classes and athletic activities. With the pandemic causing the institution to adapt to remote learning, it has been a unique experience meeting others through classes. Similarly, the past track and cross country seasons have both been cancelled due to the pandemic. Although there has not been meets, the team has been practicing social distancing and mandatory mask protocols in order to quarantining in a pseudo-bubble here in Davis. This has permitted my teammates and I to continue training and partcipating in team hosted time trials. So while competition has been cancelled, the grind still continues. I am grateful to be able to run, as it allows me to get out of the house once in a while, taking a break from my Zoom classes.

Besides running and programming, hobbies of mine include playing chess, playing League of Legends, cooking (particularly East Asian dishes), photography, and beekeeping.
