My Hobbies and Interests

Right now, I devote most of my time to coursework and endurance running. I run track and cross country for the NCAA team here at UC Davis in addition to pursuing my bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

This is my third year at UC Davis and I am incredibly grateful to have met great friends through my classes and athletic activities. Despite the pandemic, I have made an extra effort to reach out to connect with others and maintain structure in my life!

Besides running and programming, hobbies of mine include playing chess, playing League of Legends, cooking (particularly East Asian dishes), photography, and beekeeping. I hope to continue updating my hobbies and interest pages as you can tell this website is a Work-In-Progress. Nonetheless, thank you so much for taking the time to learn more about me!
